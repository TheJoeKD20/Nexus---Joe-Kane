## What is Nexus?

**Nexus is a self-hosted, real-time support wallboard** for service desks, MSPs and IT teams. It connects to the tools you already run — **Zendesk, Freshdesk, Freshservice, Jira Service Management, HaloPSA, SuperOps, NinjaOne and ConnectWise PSA** — and puts every ticket, call and SLA on one live board the whole room can read at a glance.

No cloud middleman, no data leaving your network: Nexus runs **on your own machine, server or Docker host**. You point it at your help desk, pick your panels, and the board updates in real time.

> **Why "wallboard"?** Because support that everyone can *see* is support everyone can *act on* — fewer breached SLAs, faster pickups, and a team that knows exactly where the queue stands without asking.

---

## ✨ Key features

- **📊 34 live panels** — tickets, calls, SLAs, KPIs, leaderboards, war-room views and more, arranged however your team works.
- **🔌 Multi-provider** — Zendesk (Support + Talk), Freshdesk, Freshservice, Jira Service Management, HaloPSA, SuperOps, NinjaOne and ConnectWise PSA.
- **🖥️ Monitoring built in** — pull device, backup and security status from your RMM alongside the ticket queue.
- **📺 TV mode** — a clean, glanceable layout designed for the big screen on the office wall.
- **🎨 11 themes** — from understated Midnight to bold, branded looks, with live colour theming.
- **🌍 Everywhere** — macOS, Windows, Linux, Docker, iOS and Android. One board, every device.
- **🔒 Self-hosted & private** — your data stays on your infrastructure; telemetry is minimal and **opt-out**.

## 🚀 Get started

1. **Try the [live demo](https://nexus.joekane.org/demo)** — everything unlocked, nothing to install.
2. **[Download Nexus](https://nexus.joekane.org/releases)** for your platform, or pull the Docker image for self-hosting.
3. **Connect your help desk** (Zendesk, Freshdesk, HaloPSA, …) with a one-time activation token.
4. **Pick your panels and theme**, cast it to the office TV, and you're live.

Full setup walkthrough in the **[documentation](https://nexus.joekane.org/docs)**.
